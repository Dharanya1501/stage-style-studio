

# Stage Decor E-Commerce Platform

## Overview
A professional website for a stage decoration business, showcasing physical products, rental services, and custom design offerings. The site will feature an elegant, luxurious aesthetic with dark tones and gold accents — fitting for the events and decor industry.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Homepage
- Hero section with a stunning full-width image/video of stage decor work
- Tagline and call-to-action buttons ("Browse Products" / "Request a Quote")
- Highlighted categories: Physical Products, Rentals, Custom Designs
- Featured/popular products carousel
- Testimonials from past clients
- Stats section (events completed, happy clients, etc.)

### 2. Product Catalog
- Grid layout with filtering by category (backdrops, lighting, florals, props, fabrics, etc.)
- Search functionality
- Each product card shows image, name, price, and "Add to Cart" or "Inquire" button
- Product detail page with image gallery, description, pricing, and related items

### 3. Rental Services Page
- Browse available rental items with daily/event pricing
- Rental terms and conditions
- Inquiry form to request rental availability and quotes

### 4. Custom Design Services Page
- Portfolio gallery showcasing past custom stage designs
- Description of the custom design process (consultation → design → setup)
- Quote request form (event type, date, budget range, vision/theme)

### 5. Shopping Cart & Checkout
- Cart with quantity management
- Contact/shipping information form
- Order summary and confirmation page
- *(Stripe or Shopify integration for payments can be added when ready)*

### 6. About Us
- Brand story and mission
- Team introduction
- Why choose us

### 7. Contact Page
- Contact form (name, email, phone, message)
- Business location map
- Phone, email, and social media links
- WhatsApp quick-contact button

### 8. Navigation & Footer
- Clean top navigation with logo, menu links, cart icon
- Footer with quick links, social media, newsletter signup

---

## Design Style
- **Elegant & luxurious**: Dark background with gold/champagne accents
- High-quality imagery emphasis
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)

---

## Starting Approach
- All product data will be stored locally in the app (no backend needed initially)
- Inquiry forms will use toast notifications to confirm submission
- Backend (Supabase for database, Stripe/Shopify for payments) can be connected later when you're ready to process real orders
